If you were notified of possible COVID-19 exposure via the California COVID Notify app, please do the following:

If you’re at UCSD

  • Call (XXX) XXX-XXXX to get tested
  • Quarantine to protect others. Learn how.
  • Check UCSD’s COVID-19 policies and guidance

If you’re at UCSF

  • Call (415) 514-7666 to get tested
  • Quarantine to protect others. Learn how.
  • Check UCSF’s COVID-19 policies and guidance

If you have COVID-19 symptoms, such as fever, cough, trouble breathing, or loss of taste or smell, self-isolate and seek medical attention immediately. Check your symptoms.

About the app

California COVID Notify quickly notifies you if you may have been exposed to COVID-19. You can then protect your friends and family, reducing the spread of infection in your community.

The State of California is partnering with the University of California to test the app in a limited pilot. The results will determine whether the app will be used among all Californians.

Thanks for your help in keeping our communities safe.
